# Statutory Interpretation Guide

A comprehensive guide for reading, interpreting, and applying statutes in legal contexts.

## When to use this skill

Use this skill when the user asks about:
- How to read and interpret statutes
- Statutory interpretation methods and canons
- Understanding legislative intent
- Applying statutes to specific legal situations
- Legal analysis involving statutory language

## What's included

This skill contains a 13-page guide on statutory interpretation by Katharine Clark, covering:

- Principles of statutory construction
- Methods for determining legislative intent
- Canons of interpretation
- Practical examples of statutory analysis

It also includes an **Extraction Playbook** (`references/extraction_playbook.md`) with a concise, actionable checklist for extracting requirements from privacy/tech statutes — covering citation rules, classification criteria, consolidation rules, and common pitfalls.
